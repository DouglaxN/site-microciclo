About

MicroCiclo is a startup that treats oil-contaminated waste using bioremediation eating oil from industrial waste using microorganisms. We want to change the way oil is treated around the world.

MicroCiclo is a biotechnological academic spin off, founded in August 2019, by researchers from the Federal University of Rio Grande do Norte (UFRN).
During fifteen years of research, in the areas of environmental genetics and petroleum microbiology, employing molecular biology, microbiology, biochemistry and bioinformatics techniques, the multidisciplinary team of the Molecular and Genomic Biology Laboratory (LBMG), selected bacteria capable of consuming the hydrocarbons of the petroleum and industrial oils.

Product

MicroCiclo develops mixtures of non-pathogenic bacteria to degrade petroleum fractions (diesel, gasoline, lubricating oils, toxic to human and environmental health). Bacterial formulations are produced in liquid form for immediate use or in powder for conditioning at room temperature.

Product's name Application Target Audience Advantages/benefits
MC1 Oily residue Metallurgical Reduction of oil and grease content
MC2 Oily residue Metallurgical Reduction of oil and grease content


The MicroCiclo product is safe, with bacteria from natural environments and without genetic modifications. The products are designed to treat oily waste from industries such as metallurgy, steel and gas stations.

The proof of concept showed 60% of degradation of oil waste from a gas washer in only six days! MicroCiclo also seeks to promote practicality, enabling application within industry facilities such as containers, oil and water separators, gas washers or in containment ponds. Depending on the waste type, specific products can be designed, in a modality of co-development of the solution.
